Institutional History

A Legacy of Precision.

Established on 23 June 1980, Army Public School Dinjan began as a dedicated primary wing. Over the decades, under the guidance of the Army Welfare Education Society (AWES), it has evolved into a premier co-educational Senior Secondary institution.

Operating across a sprawling 20,234.28 sq. meter campus constructed in 2010, the institution natively serves the wards of defense personnel while welcoming discerning civilian minds. We provide holistic, English-medium education governed by the strict academic rigor of the CBSE curriculum.

"Our absolute focus remains on unyielding discipline, profound core values, academic rigor, and the holistic personality development of every cadet under our care."

— Mrs. Krishna Baruah, Principal
